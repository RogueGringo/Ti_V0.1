### Moonshot Conditions

The moonshot scenario requires:
1. ATFT becomes the standard framework for sheaf-valued TDA (analogous to how TensorFlow/PyTorch became standard for neural networks)
2. AI interpretability vertical achieves product-market fit with frontier labs
3. Financial signal detection produces measurable alpha
4. Defense contracts established
5. The mathematical framework yields a publishable advance toward the Riemann Hypothesis (scientific prestige drives adoption)

**Probability estimate: 5-10%.** But the expected value at 5% probability and 50x return is 2.5x invested capital, which is attractive for a deep-tech seed.

## 5. Why Now

1. **AI interpretability demand is exploding.** Regulatory pressure (EU AI Act, US executive orders) creates mandatory demand for model analysis tools. ATFT's cross-model topology correlation (r=0.9998) is directly applicable.

2. **GPU compute is commoditizing.** The matrix-free engine runs on consumer GPUs. Cloud GPU pricing continues to fall. The compute moat is in the algorithm, not the hardware.

3. **TDA is crossing the chasm.** After 15 years as academic curiosity, topological data analysis is being adopted by pharma (drug discovery), finance (risk), and defense (pattern recognition). ATFT enters a warming market.

4. **AI-augmented research is validated.** This codebase proves that one expert with AI tools can produce research-grade software at 10x traditional speed. The investment thesis is not just the framework---it's the demonstration that deep-tech can be built lean.

5. **The Riemann Hypothesis angle is a PR multiplier.** Whether or not ATFT contributes to proving RH, the narrative ("a framework that detects structure in the primes that no other method can see") drives outsized media attention relative to investment. This is free customer acquisition.
